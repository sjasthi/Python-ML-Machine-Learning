üìñ Understanding the Metrics:

MSE (Mean Squared Error): Average of squared residuals. Lower = better fit.

RMSE (Root MSE): Square root of MSE, in original units. Easier to interpret.

R¬≤ Score: Ranges 0-1. Closer to 1.0 = better fit. Shows % of variance explained.
