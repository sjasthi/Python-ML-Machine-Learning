ğŸ¯ Why Use Cross-Validation?

When creating meta-features, we use cross-validation to avoid overfitting:

  • Split training data into 5 folds
  • For each fold: train on 4, predict on 1
  • Collect all predictions as meta-features
  • Train meta-model on these
