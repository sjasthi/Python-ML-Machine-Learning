## Overview

This week marks the transition from learning algorithms to **applying them**. Students explore certifications they can earn, project ideas rooted in what they have built over 27 weeks, and a full glossary of ML terms to use as a reference throughout the final project phase.

---

## Learning Goals

By the end of this week, students will be able to:

- Identify at least one ML certification they can pursue right now
- Describe a project idea connected to at least one algorithm from the course
- Use the ML Glossary to look up and explain any key term from the course
- Understand how to find datasets, host projects, and enter competitions
