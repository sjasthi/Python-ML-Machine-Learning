## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

### **Q: Can I use code from Claude directly?**
**A:** Yes, but you must:
- Understand what it does
- Test that it works
- Add your own comments
- Customize it to fit your resource
- Document it in your usage log

### **Q: How much of my resource can come from Claude?**
**A:** Claude can help with structure, code examples, and explanations, but:
- YOU choose the analogies (or customize Claude's)
- YOU organize the content
- YOU add personal examples
- YOU make it engaging and creative
- Aim for at least 30% original content

### **Q: What if my code doesn't work?**
**A:** 
- Test it early!
- Ask Claude: "This code gives error X, how do I fix it?"
- Ask classmates or instructor for help
- Debug step by step
- Document the problem and solution in your usage log

### **Q: Can I work with a partner?**
**A:** No, this is an individual assignment. However:
- You can discuss ideas with classmates
- You can peer-review each other's work
- You cannot share Claude prompts or responses
- Your final resource must be your own

### **Q: How long should this take?**
**A:** Plan for 4-6 hours total:
- 1 hour: Planning and research
- 2-3 hours: Creating content with Claude
- 1 hour: Testing and refining
- 30 min: Creating usage log
- 30 min: Final review

### **Q: What makes a resource "creative"?**
**A:** Creative means:
- Original analogies or examples
- Unique presentation style
- Interactive or visual elements
- Personal voice and humor
- Going beyond basic explanations

### **Q: Can I include memes or jokes?**
**A:** Yes! As long as they are:
- Appropriate for school
- Relevant to the topic
- Enhance learning (not just for laughs)
- Original or properly credited
